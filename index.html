About Us

Villa del Prado and Beach resort, situated in the exclusive Southern Tagalog Region of Quezon Province, has the kind of facilities and services that can cater to your every whim.The resort offers a host of different accommodations taking advantage of the lively ambience provided by the Villa del Prado Resort. You can bathe under the sun in the softest, finest stretch of sand beach at Villa del Prado beach resort located along the coast of Tayabas Bay.
